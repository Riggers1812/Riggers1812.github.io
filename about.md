# About Me

## Building Systems Behind Better Decisions

Hello, I'm David Rigby.

I'm a Business Intelligence Developer and Data & Analytics Builder who enjoys helping organisations turn data into better decisions.

My work sits at the intersection of business, technology, and analytics. I design and develop data solutions that transform complex information into practical insights, helping teams understand performance, identify opportunities, and make confident decisions.

Over the years, I have developed skills across SQL, BigQuery, Looker, LookML, Python, Power BI, Data Modelling, and Analytics Engineering. While I enjoy working with tools and technology, I believe the real value of analytics comes from solving business problems and creating systems that people trust and use.

## What I Focus On

I enjoy building:

- Reliable data models that provide a trusted source of truth
- Scalable reporting and analytics solutions
- Self-service analytics that empower users
- Automated processes that reduce manual effort
- Data products that create measurable business value

My approach is simple:

> Use data to make life easier for people and decisions better for businesses.

## My Philosophy
